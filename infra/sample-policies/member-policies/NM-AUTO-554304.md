## Policy Form Clauses

### §3.4 Lapse and Reinstatement

If any premium installment is not paid by its due date, this policy shall lapse and all coverage shall terminate at 12:01 a.m. on the day following the due date specified in the most recent premium notice. Reinstatement of a lapsed policy may be granted at our sole discretion upon receipt of (a) a written reinstatement application from the named insured, (b) full payment of all outstanding premium plus any reinstatement fee, and (c) a no-known-loss certification signed by the named insured certifying that no loss has occurred during the period of lapse. **Reinstatement, if granted, is effective only on the date and time we approve the application; it is not retroactive.** Any loss occurring during the period of lapse, including any loss occurring after the lapse date and before our approval of reinstatement, is not covered under this policy.

### §3.5 Renewal

This policy will be offered for renewal subject to our underwriting review and any applicable premium adjustment. A renewal offer, when issued, will be sent to the address shown on the Declarations Page no later than thirty (30) calendar days prior to the expiration date. Failure of the insured to remit the renewal premium by the expiration date will result in lapse per §3.4.

### §17.1 Cooperation Clause

The insured shall cooperate with us in the investigation of any matter related to this policy, including any inquiry into a loss reported after policy expiration. Such cooperation does not constitute admission of coverage.
