## Policy Form Clauses

### §4.3 Comprehensive (Other Than Collision) Coverage

We will pay for direct and accidental loss to a covered auto, including its equipment, less the applicable deductible shown on the Declarations Page, caused by any cause other than collision with another object or upset.

### §7.2 Required Documentation for Comprehensive Losses

For any loss covered under Section 4.3, the insured shall file a written report with the appropriate law enforcement agency in any case of theft, larceny, vandalism, or malicious mischief; submit photographs of the loss; permit us to inspect the damaged property prior to repair or disposal; and provide repair estimates from one or more qualified repair providers.

### §9.1 Excluded Uses (As Modified by Rideshare Endorsement)

Standard exclusion (a) — "while being used to carry persons or property for a fee, including but not limited to delivery driving, ride-hailing, or transportation network company (TNC) operation" — is **modified by Form CM-RSE-22**. See §CM-RSE-22 below for the substituted language.

### §11.1 Total Loss Threshold

A covered auto shall be deemed a constructive total loss when the cost to repair the auto to its pre-loss condition equals or exceeds eighty percent (80%) of the actual cash value of the auto at the time of loss.

### §11.4 Repair vs Total Loss Decision

When a covered auto is damaged but the cost of repair does not meet the total-loss threshold under §11.1, we will pay the lesser of (a) the cost of repair using parts of like kind, quality, and condition less the applicable deductible, or (b) the actual cash value of the auto at the time of loss less the applicable deductible.

### §CM-RSE-22 Rideshare Driver Endorsement

This Endorsement modifies §9.1 with respect to the covered auto identified in the Declarations Page as eligible. The standard exclusion for use to carry persons or property for a fee (including transportation network company operation) is hereby DELETED with respect to this covered auto. In its place, the following applies: We will provide Comprehensive, Collision, Liability, Uninsured Motorist, and Medical Payments coverage during all phases of TNC operation, including (i) the period when the TNC mobile application is on and the driver is awaiting a match, (ii) the period from match acceptance to passenger pickup, and (iii) the period from passenger pickup to passenger drop-off. This coverage is excess over any coverage provided by the TNC company during periods (ii) and (iii); during period (i), this policy is primary. Commercial use other than TNC operation (delivery for a fee, hauling for a fee, livery service unrelated to a TNC platform) remains excluded.

### §CM-AMEND-NM-2024 New Mexico Mandatory Amendatory Endorsement

This Endorsement amends the policy to comply with New Mexico Insurance Code §59A-16 et seq.

### §17.1 Cooperation Clause

The insured shall cooperate with us in the investigation, settlement, and defense of any claim.
