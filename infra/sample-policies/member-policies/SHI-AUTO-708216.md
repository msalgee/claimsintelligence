## Comprehensive Coverage - Weather and Hail

Summit Heritage will pay for direct and accidental loss to the covered auto caused by missiles or falling objects, fire, theft, larceny, explosion, earthquake, windstorm, hail, water, flood, vandalism, riot or civil commotion, contact with bird or animal, or breakage of glass. For policy SHI-AUTO-708216, the comprehensive deductible for VIN JTMRWRFV5RD082914 is $500.

## Glass Coverage - Endorsement SHI-GLASS-CO-07

Endorsement SHI-GLASS-CO-07 applies to the covered auto. Windshield and safety-glass repair or replacement is handled under Comprehensive Other Than Collision when caused by hail, falling objects, road debris, vandalism, or other covered comprehensive causes. Advanced driver assistance system recalibration is included when required by the repair procedure for covered windshield replacement. The comprehensive deductible shown in the declarations applies unless a separate glass-only waiver is shown; no separate waiver is shown for this policy.

## OEM Parts - Endorsement SHI-OEM-24

Endorsement SHI-OEM-24 applies to the 2024 Toyota RAV4 XLE Hybrid while the vehicle is within 36 months of the original new-vehicle purchase date. When repair or replacement parts are required for a covered loss, Summit Heritage will authorize original equipment manufacturer parts where commercially available and reasonable for the repair scope.

## Weather Loss Documentation - Endorsement SHI-WTHR-01

For windstorm, hail, flood, or falling-object losses, the insured must provide reasonable documentation of the date, location, and cause of loss. Acceptable documentation includes National Weather Service storm reports, law-enforcement or municipal incident reports, repair facility inspection notes, date-stamped photographs, or other reliable evidence tying the vehicle damage to the reported weather event.

## Loss Settlement Conditions

Payment for physical damage to the covered auto is limited to the amount necessary to repair or replace damaged property with like kind and quality, the actual cash value of the damaged property at the time of loss, or the limit shown in the policy, whichever is less. The applicable deductible is subtracted from any covered physical damage settlement.

## Claim Cooperation Duties

The insured must cooperate with Summit Heritage in the investigation, inspection, appraisal, and settlement of a claim. The insured must protect the covered auto from further damage, allow inspection before repairs when reasonably requested, and provide records or statements needed to verify coverage and amount of loss.
