## Policy Form Clauses

### Section 4.3 Comprehensive (Other Than Collision) Coverage

Northwind Mutual will pay for direct and accidental loss to a covered auto, including its equipment, less the applicable deductible shown on the Declarations Page, caused by any cause other than collision with another object or upset. Covered comprehensive causes include theft or larceny, attempted theft, malicious mischief or vandalism, fire, explosion, windstorm, hail, flood, contact with a bird or animal, falling objects, and breakage of glass. Recovery of a stolen vehicle does not eliminate coverage for covered theft-related damage discovered at recovery.

### Section 7.2 Required Documentation for Comprehensive Losses

For theft, attempted theft, vandalism, malicious mischief, or glass losses, the insured must file a police report or provide the police case number when reasonably available; provide photographs of the damaged property; permit inspection before repairs when requested; and provide repair estimates, invoices, or other records needed to verify the covered damage and amount of loss. Failure to provide requested documentation after a reasonable opportunity to comply may delay payment or affect coverage.

### Section 11.4 Repair Settlement

When a covered auto is damaged but repair is economically practical, Northwind Mutual will pay the reasonable cost to repair the covered damage with parts of like kind and quality, subject to any applicable endorsement, less the applicable deductible. Payment may be made jointly to the insured and lienholder or directly to an approved repair facility when authorized by the insured and permitted by law.

### Endorsement NMI-OEM-08 OEM Parts

For any covered partial loss to Vehicle 1, original equipment manufacturer parts are authorized where commercially available and reasonable for the repair scope. If OEM parts are unavailable within fourteen calendar days, Northwind Mutual will consult the insured before authorizing alternative parts.

### Endorsement NMI-GLASS-WA-12 Glass Repair and Recalibration

Covered glass repair or replacement for Vehicle 1 includes reasonable calibration of windshield-mounted cameras or sensors when calibration is required by the repair procedure. Glass replacement remains subject to the Comprehensive deductible unless a separate deductible waiver appears on the Declarations Page; no separate waiver is shown for this policy.

### Endorsement NMI-AMEND-WA-2025 Washington Mandatory Amendatory Endorsement

This endorsement amends the policy to comply with Washington insurance law. Northwind Mutual must acknowledge claim communications promptly, explain any additional information needed to complete the investigation, and provide a clear coverage decision once required documentation is received. Where any policy provision conflicts with Washington law, Washington law controls.

### Section 17.1 Cooperation Clause

The insured must cooperate with Northwind Mutual in the investigation, inspection, appraisal, and settlement of a claim. Cooperation includes providing statements, records, keys, photographs, repair estimates, police report information, and access to the damaged vehicle when reasonably requested.
