## Policy Form Clauses

### §4.1 Per-Vehicle Liability Limit

Liability coverage applies separately to each covered auto shown on the Declarations Page. The limit shown for any one auto is the most we will pay as a result of any one accident involving that auto, regardless of the number of insureds, claims made, vehicles or premiums shown on the Declarations Page, or persons injured.

### §4.3 Comprehensive (Other Than Collision) Coverage

We will pay for direct and accidental loss to a covered auto, including its equipment, less the applicable deductible shown on the Declarations Page, caused by any cause other than collision with another object or upset. Loss caused by missiles or falling objects, fire, theft or larceny, explosion or earthquake, windstorm, hail, water or flood, malicious mischief or vandalism, contact with a bird or animal, or breakage of glass shall be covered under this Comprehensive coverage. The most we will pay for loss in any one occurrence is the actual cash value of the stolen, damaged, or destroyed property at the time of loss less the applicable deductible.

### §7.2 Required Documentation for Comprehensive Losses

For any loss covered under Section 4.3 Comprehensive coverage, the insured shall, as soon as reasonably practicable, file a written report of the loss with the appropriate law enforcement agency in any case of theft, larceny, vandalism, or malicious mischief; submit photographs depicting the loss; permit us to inspect the damaged property prior to its repair or disposal; and provide repair estimates from one or more qualified repair providers.

### §9.1 Excluded Uses

We do not provide Comprehensive, Collision, or Liability coverage for any covered auto: (a) while being used to carry persons or property for a fee, including delivery driving or transportation network company (TNC) operation; (b) while being used in any prearranged racing; (c) for any loss arising out of intentional acts of the insured.

### §11.1 Total Loss Threshold

A covered auto shall be deemed a constructive total loss when the cost to repair the auto to its pre-loss condition equals or exceeds eighty percent (80%) of the actual cash value of the auto at the time of loss.

### §11.4 Repair vs Total Loss Decision

When a covered auto is damaged but the cost of repair does not meet the total-loss threshold under §11.1, we will pay the lesser of (a) the cost of repair using parts of like kind, quality, and condition less the applicable deductible, or (b) the actual cash value of the auto at the time of loss less the applicable deductible. Because no OEM Parts Endorsement is in force on this policy, we may, at our option, specify the use of aftermarket, recycled, or remanufactured parts in any repair authorization, provided such parts are functionally equivalent to OEM parts.

### §CM-AMEND-NM-2024 New Mexico Mandatory Amendatory Endorsement

This Endorsement amends the policy to comply with New Mexico Insurance Code §59A-16 et seq.

### §17.1 Cooperation Clause

The insured shall cooperate with us in the investigation, settlement, and defense of any claim, including providing recorded or written statements, producing documents, permitting inspection of damaged property, and submitting to a reasonable Examination Under Oath when warranted.
