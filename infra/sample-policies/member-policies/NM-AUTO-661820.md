## Policy Form Clauses

### Section 3.1 Permitted Use of the Covered Auto

The covered auto is rated, priced, and insured for the use class shown on the Declarations Page. The use class on this policy is **"Pleasure / commute (personal use)"**, which means use by the named insured and listed drivers for personal errands, social and recreational driving, commuting between residence and a single regular place of employment, and incidental personal trips. Operation of the covered auto for any other purpose at the time of loss is governed by Section IV (Exclusions) and may result in denial of coverage for that loss.

### Section 4.2 Collision Coverage

Northwind Mutual will pay for direct and accidental loss to a covered auto caused by impact of the covered auto with another vehicle or object, or by upset of the covered auto, less the applicable deductible shown on the Declarations Page, **subject to all exclusions in Section IV** and provided the covered auto was being used at the time of loss in a manner permitted by Section 3.1.

### Section IV - Exclusions Applicable to All Vehicle Damage Coverages

This policy **does not provide coverage** for any loss arising out of, during, or while the covered auto is being used for any of the following:

- (a) **Carrying persons or property for a fee, charge, or other compensation**, including but not limited to taxi, livery, limousine, shuttle, jitney, or any similar passenger-for-hire operation.
- (b) **Transportation network company (TNC) services**, including but not limited to ride-hailing, ride-share, on-demand passenger transport, and any service in which a passenger is matched to the driver through a digital platform or mobile application, **regardless of whether the platform application is actively logged in at the time of loss**.
- (c) **Delivery of food, parcels, packages, groceries, prepared meals, or other goods for a fee, charge, or other compensation**, including but not limited to gig-economy delivery platforms, courier services, and contract delivery work.
- (d) **Any other compensation-for-transport activity** by which the named insured, a listed driver, or any other operator receives or expects to receive compensation, fees, tips, gratuities, platform credits, mileage-based reimbursement beyond IRS personal mileage, or other consideration in exchange for the operation of the covered auto.

This Section IV exclusion applies whether or not a passenger or cargo was actually being transported for compensation at the precise moment of loss; it is sufficient that the covered auto was being held out for, prepared for, returning from, or otherwise engaged in any of the activities listed in (a)-(d) at the time of loss.

This Section IV exclusion may be modified or removed only by an endorsement to this policy that is rated, priced, and listed on the Declarations Page. **No such endorsement is in force on this policy.**

### Section 7.1 Required Documentation for Collision Losses

For any collision loss, the insured must file a police or incident report or provide the case number when reasonably available; provide photographs of the damaged property; permit inspection before repairs when requested; and provide repair estimates, invoices, and other records needed to verify the covered damage and amount of loss. The insured must also provide a truthful description of the time, place, circumstances, **and purpose of the trip** during which the loss occurred.

### Section 11.4 Repair Settlement

When a covered auto is damaged but repair is economically practical, Northwind Mutual will pay the reasonable cost to repair the covered damage with parts of like kind and quality, less the applicable deductible. **Pre-existing modifications, fittings, or accessories not declared on the Declarations Page or not consistent with the declared Use Class** (for example, livery roof signs, taxi placard mounts, ride-share trade-dress decals, after-market dual-cradle commercial phone-mount rigs, or commercial dashcams associated with for-hire operation) **are not covered for repair, removal, refinishing, or detail work, and any cost associated with such items will be excluded from the repair settlement.**

### Section 17.1 Cooperation Clause

The insured must cooperate with Northwind Mutual in the investigation, inspection, appraisal, and settlement of a claim. Cooperation includes providing statements, records, photographs, repair estimates, police report information, **truthful description of the purpose of the trip**, and access to the damaged vehicle when reasonably requested.
